## AI & Tech

- **China's new AI-companion law took effect July 15**, forcing ByteDance's Doubao and Alibaba's Qwen to shut down their personalized agent features. Doubao is giving users a read-only export window on their chat histories until October 15, while Qwen has announced no grace period and will permanently delete agent configurations and conversations. [TechTimes](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/319703/20260704/china-ai-companion-law-arrives-july-15-doubao-qwen-agent-data-will-deleted.htm)
