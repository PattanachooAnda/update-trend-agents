# Daily News Digest — 2026-07-15 (10:00 snapshot)

## Crypto & Markets

- **Trump reversed course on the proposed 20% Hormuz transit fee, calming markets.** A day after floating the surprise cargo charge for ships transiting the Strait of Hormuz under the reinstated US blockade of Iran, Trump said the US would not impose transit fees after all, opting instead for Trade and Investment Deals with Gulf States — oil prices steadied on the news. [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/07/14/nx-s1-5893257/us-iran-updates)
- **Asian equities rallied broadly on cooler US inflation data**, with MSCI's Asia Pacific gauge up 1.6% and tech stocks leading; South Korea's Kospi jumped nearly 6%, with SK Hynix shares up 11% (ADRs up 27%) on AI-chip demand. [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-07-14/asian-stocks-poised-to-track-us-gains-on-soft-cpi-markets-wrap)
- **Bitcoin and Ethereum extended their rebound**, with Bitcoin trading in the low-$60,000s and Ethereum up sharply on the week as softer June CPI eased Fed rate-hike expectations, though crypto sentiment gauges still read "Extreme Fear" amid lingering Middle East risk. [Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/coverage/stock-market-today/2026/07/14/crypto-market-today-july-14-ethereum-soars-6-on-cooler-inflation-data/)

[Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/indices/thailand-set)

## AI & Tech

- **China's new AI-companion law took effect today (July 15), forcing ByteDance's Doubao and Alibaba's Qwen to shut down personalized agent features.** Doubao is offering a read-only chat-history export window through October 15, while Qwen is deleting agent configurations and conversations immediately with no grace period. [TechTimes](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/319703/20260704/china-ai-companion-law-arrives-july-15-doubao-qwen-agent-data-will-deleted.htm)
- **Applications for Anthropic's Claude for Science grants closed today**, with roughly 50 research projects to split up to $30,000 each in API/compute credits; award notifications are due July 31, with funded work running September–December. [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-science-ai-workbench)
- **AI stocks led the Asian market rally** as chip and AI-infrastructure names (notably SK Hynix) surged on renewed demand momentum tied to the broader AI trade, coinciding with continued frontier-model competition between Anthropic, OpenAI, and rivals following recent releases of Claude Sonnet 5, OpenAI's Sol/Terra/Luna and GPT-5.6, and xAI's Grok 4.5. [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-07-14/asian-stocks-poised-to-track-us-gains-on-soft-cpi-markets-wrap)

## Thailand

- **Investigators continue probing the Lat Phrao pub fire that killed 30**, focusing on flammable foam soundproofing, a bolted rear exit near where most victims died, and whether the venue — which lacked a valid entertainment license — had obstructed emergency exits; Chatuchak district has issued a 30-day closure order. [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/14/bangkok-fire-death-toll-rises-to-30-as-police-probe-possible-negligence)
- **PM Anutin Charnvirakul ordered a nationwide 30-day inspection of entertainment venues and buildings**, directing the Interior Ministry, Bangkok Metropolitan Administration, and police to check fire-prevention systems, exits, occupancy limits, and legal compliance, with non-compliant venues shut down immediately. [Xinhua](https://english.news.cn/20260714/7bd0508fb8434633afaa3e18df1ebbf3/c.html)
- **The SET index and baht stayed range-bound in early Wednesday trading**, with the central bank continuing to watch Middle East-driven volatility without intervening. [Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/indices/thailand-set)
