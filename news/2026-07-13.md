## Thailand

- **Bangkok pub fire kills 27, injures dozens; negligence probe underway.** A blaze tore through the Rong Beer Na Lat Phrao venue near Lat Phrao Soi 1 just before midnight, killing 27 and injuring between 18 and 63 people depending on the source. Police believe an electrical short circuit in a ceiling air conditioner may have started the fire, and investigators found emergency exits obstructed by beer crates and a sweets table; national police chief Kitrat Panphet said negligence is the primary investigative theory. [Khaosod English](https://www.khaosodenglish.com/featured/2026/07/13/bangkok-pub-fire-kills-27-as-obstructed-exits-come-under-scrutiny/) / [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/12/fire-engulfs-popular-bangkok-pub-killing-27-people-and-injuring-63)

[Thaiger](https://thethaiger.com/video-podcasts/thailand-video-news-13-07-2026)
